Today we are excited to formally launch The Wire China’s News Products business at reports.thewirechina.com.

Our News Products site contains our company deep dives, China Files, and our guides to the top 100 people in an industry in China, Who's Who. 

Subscribers to the Wire receive a 40% discount on all our news products. Send an email to newsproducts@thewire.media after purchase of your subscription to receive your discount code. 
Subscribe to The Wire China
New additions to our news products include a China File on Disney in China and a China File on one of China’s first private car companies, Geely. We have also published a guide to the top 100 people in the Gambling Industry in Asia, as well as a guide to President Biden’s China Team. 

China Files contain our best open-source research on a company. We include corporate registration documents, public filings, presentations, academic papers, patents, press releases, social media, and court documents. Where relevant, we’ll also insert material from our own archives, documents gathered by reporters at The Wire.  

Who's Who is a list of 100 of the most influential people in a given industry focused on China. In addition to executives, we feature others who help make an industry tick — advisors, academics, consultants, regulators, service providers, and power brokers that operate at the intersection between China and the rest of the world. 

We are also offering a 50% discount on our news products for journalists, scholars, universities and non-profit organizations. Please email us for a discount code at newsproducts@thewire.media with evidence of your eligibility. 

As always, if you have any feedback, you can reach us at contact@thewire.media or write directly to me at david@thewire.media

Please enjoy.
