Second, I've been trying to read more fiction so I asked on Twitter for everyone's favorite Sci-Fi recommendations and got a fantastic list. First, a few of mine:

The Three-Body Problem Trilogy. This is probably the best "hard" Sci-Fi I've read. Fantastic story that really builds through the third book, and it had me wanting to go to bed an hour earlier just so I had more time to read it.

The Wool Trilogy. Exciting post-apocalyptic sci-fi. Great if you ever played the "Fallout" games.

Snow Crash. This is basically the OG "Ready Player One." Snow Crash is fun to read since it's so old and still gets quite a few things right, and it's just wonderfully written. Ready Player One is a fun fast-paced read too if you haven't checked it out.
