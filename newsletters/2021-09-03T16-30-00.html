Immutable Retroactive Rewards
 


 

Immutable X is a next-generation protocol for trading Ethereum NFTs which give users instant trade confirmation, massive scalability, and no gas fees without compromising user custody. Immutable\u2019s goal is to make minting and trading Ethereum NFTs even easier than trading traditional digital assets. For additional information on Immutable X, you can read about it here.

Alpha Rewards

400,000 IMX will be distributed to users retroactively. For clarity, users can earn both rewards from Retrospective and Alpha. To be eligible for Alpha rewards, you must complete all three tasks before the snapshot date:

1. Play at least 1 multiplayer game of Gods Unchained (Direct Challenge or Ranked)
2. Have your wallet connected to your Gods Unchained account and linked to Immutable X
3. Hold Gods Unchained NFT in your Immutable X wallet

The snapshot date hasn\u2019t been announced and it will be communicated at least one week in advance \u2013 so follow Immutable\u2019s socials to stay in the loop!

Latest Update: 30 August 2021

To reward those who have helped Immutable X grow, Immutable are announcing the multi-stage Play to Earn campaign with rewards worth up to 600,000 IMX. TO celebrate the dedication of early users that have provided support and feedback during the earliest stage of Immutable X\u2019s development, they\u2019ll be eligible for this exclusive campaign.

Note: US users who are restricted from receiving their Retrospective or Alpha rewards will be rewarded in ETH rather than IMX. You can read the updated and full version of this campaign here.

Grants Pool For Builders

There is a pool of 5000 tokens available for everyone building on Immutable X this quarter that will be divided in two ways:

1. Each project that launches on Immutable X (as measured by minting at least one NFT on mainnet Ethereum via Immutable X) will split 50% of the pool (2,500 IMX) equally.

2. The remaining 50% is attributed proportionally to the volume of primary sales each project achieves through the Immutable X Marketplace or any application powered by Immutable X. If you use Immutable X to mint on mainnet for the first time this quarter, then you are eligible for the sales pool in this quarter.

Additional Grants

In addition to the pool, if you think you\u2019re building something exceptional, or are with a large pre-existing company or NFT project \u2013 there\u2019s another 20,000 tokens in grants available for this quarter to be allocated to the most exciting partners building on Immutable X. The more detail you can fill your application with, the better.

For additional information on Token Grants and Eligibility, you can read about it here.
