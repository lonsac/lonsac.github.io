L1 szn: The Delphi Debrief - Delphi Digital

Nick Pappageorge 4-5 minutes

Markets enjoyed a nice bounce this week, with bitcoin up nearly 9%. However, the true standouts were other layer 1 protocols. Avalanche, Solana, and Terra saw remarkable price action—up 120%, 78%, and 75%, respectively—in just the past 7 days.

As markets turned risk-on in the past week or two, the alt L1 narrative took center stage. Avalanche, for example, announced $180M in liquidity mining incentives and attracted Aave and Curve to launch on its public blockchain. And, as we explored in this week’s Daily posts, Solana and Terra TVL surged as their prices ran up. Meanwhile, it’s unclear how much room there is for ETH to run, especially as the OpenSea-led NFT mania appears to be winding down.

​​​​​In Delphi news, we launched a beautiful new research portal this week. For members, we also have 2 can’t-miss reports on Lido and dYdX’s post-token growth. Plenty of alpha to dig through over the weekend.
