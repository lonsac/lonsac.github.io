SuperRare is decentralizing control over its NFT marketplace with its governance and curation token, RARE. The launch features a retroactive airdrop for all participants who used the SuperRare platform as of July 21st, 2021 (07/21).

These actions include:

  • Collector GMV: Distributed pro-rata to all collectors based on the amount of primary and secondary sales volume in ETH terms.

  • Collector Sales Count: Distributed pro-rata to all collectors based on the number of primary and secondary market transactions collectors made.

  • Artist GMV: Distributed pro-rata to all artists based on the amount of primary and secondary sales volume in ETH terms.

  • Artist Sales Count: Distributed pro-rata to all artists based on the number of primary and secondary market transactions collectors made.

  • Artist and Collector Badges: Even distribution for all users who claimed their SuperRare Artist or Collector Badges.

  • Fixed Artist: Fixed distribution for any verified artist on the platform that minted a SUPR NFT token.

Background

While SuperRare initially formed as a centralized marketplace for one of one NFTs, the team is decentralizing control over the platform with today’s launch.

Most notably, this includes the core team forfeiting the platform fees for primary and secondary sales and transferring that right to the community treasury. As expected, RARE tokens control the funds in the treasury.

Additionally, RARE will serve as a curation token for SuperRare’s soon-to-be-launched “Space Race”, which are community-vetted galleries for curators, collectors and community members to highlight and release work through SuperRare.

The total supply is 1,000,000,000 RARE with the following breakdown:

  • 15% for the Retroactive RAREdrop

  • 40% to the Community Treasury

  • 25% to SuperRare Labs

  • 14.5% to Investors

  • 5% to Partners

For those interested in getting involved with SuperRare, you can join the Discord here. Using Collab Land, RARE token holders will get access to exclusive channels to collaborate with other members in the community by typing !join in any public channel.

For more information, read up on the SuperRare network docs!

How to get this Alpha:

  1. Head to SuperRare.com/rare/claim and sign in! If you already have an account (you need one to be eligible for the airdrop!), you’ll be prompt to sign a transaction to login.

  1. Once you sign in, you’ll see the tokens you’re eligible to claim.

  1. In order to claim your tokens, you’ll be required to sign the terms and agreements to the Retroactive airdrop terms and conditions.

  1. Click accept and sign the transaction! You’ll be required to pay gas fees.

  1. And that’s it! Once the transaction confirms on-chain, you’ll have received your RARE. You can view your token allocation by going to your profile dashboard.
